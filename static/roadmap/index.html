01 · THESIS

Disruptive is the parent. Atlas is the upsell.

Disruptive is a full-stack growth agency that runs every owned brand on a shared internal stack. Atlas is the productized version of that stack, sold to customers outside Disruptive. The agency proves the product. The product compounds the agency.

The shape.

One brain. One operator. A fleet of agents. A shared memory mesh. The same architecture every customer gets, configured per use. Internal first. Customer Atlas is the same code, running for someone else.

Why this works.

By the time a surface is sellable, the operational scars are absorbed. We know where it breaks, what the operator wants, how it integrates. Customers buy hardened tools, not betas. Disruptive gets the moat: every customer is paying for a stack the agency itself uses.

THE ANALOGY
Atlas is to Disruptive what Claude is to Anthropic.

Disruptive is the operator-led growth agency that runs on the stack. Atlas is the operator-grade SaaS that other businesses buy when they want to run that way too.

04 · SERVER TOPOLOGY

One bot per EC2. Brain and operator are separate.

No single box hosts both the brain and a worker. Each customer-facing bot gets its own EC2 for blast-radius isolation. Brand is a logical grouping of bots, not a server boundary.

LAYER 1 · BRAIN
Atlas EC2
1 BOX
  • RunsAll Atlas brain personas (CTO, Head of Brand, Head of Performance, Head of CS, Founder) as configs on one runtime.
  • Storespgvector mesh, 5 schemas, Hive Mind. 768d embeddings.
  • ExposesAPI the Control Center reads from. Action endpoints the operator triggers.
  • ReceivesMemory writes from every agent EC2 in the fleet.
  • ConstraintAtlas brain is exempt from auto-restart. Diagnose root cause, do not self-heal.
LAYER 2 · OPERATOR
Zen · zen-control EC2
1 BOX
  • IdentityZen is the CTO operator. Persona on the brain, runtime on this box.
  • RunsCross-server SSM, deploys, diagnostics, repo ops, infrastructure changes.
  • Why separateMistakes on operator box must not take down brain. Brain must not slow under operator workloads.
  • ReachesAll bot EC2s via SSM, the brain via API, GitHub via PAT, AWS via instance role.
  • FutureStays separate through 90d. Merge candidate after Atlas customer instances stabilize.
LAYER 3 · FLEET
Per-bot EC2s
N BOXES
  • PatternOne EC2 per bot. Tommy on RAW Athletic is its own EC2. Tommy on RAW Nutrition is a different EC2. Same brain, separate runtimes.
  • BrandsBrand is a logical filter, not a server. RAW Athletic Stuart runs on the same box as RAW Athletic main, location-scoped at the data layer.
  • Functional botsCross-brand workers (brief generator, voice monitor, compliance, research) get their own EC2s and are addressed by every brand through the brain.
  • MemoryRead/write Atlas mesh via API. No local memory stores.
  • Constraint24/7 Restart=always. Customer-facing, must self-heal.
  • TodayRAW Athletic Tommy live. Other bot EC2s spin up as the 30/60/90 commitments land.
LAYER 4 · WEB SURFACE
Control Center deploy
VERCEL / GH PAGES
  • StackStatic or SSR Next.js. No own backend.
  • ReadsAtlas API for all data. Stateless. Throwaway. Re-skinnable without infra impact.
  • ViewsDisruptive HQ · per-brand · per-role. Same code, scoped data per view.
  • ProductizedCustomer Atlas ships its own Control Center deploy as part of the bundle.
LAYER 5 · CUSTOMER ATLAS · S4 / S5

14 · ATLAS · GO-TO-MARKET

Atlas is the end-all-be-all growth partner.

Operating system. Plugged into the customer's business at the orchestration layer as a fully agentic AI. Full Disruptive oversight. Comes back to Hive Mind. Configurable per business, department, or role.

The pitch.

We don't sell software. We sell our internal stack, hardened for you. Your Head of Brand gets an Atlas persona with brand-voice fingerprinting. Your CTO gets the same operator brain Disruptive uses. Your CS lead gets Tommy variants tuned to your customers. Your founder gets a Founder-tier persona that thinks across departments. All of it reading and writing a memory mesh you control. All observable through one cream-and-pine cockpit.

Configuration vectors.

Atlas configures along three axes. Pick the ones that matter for the customer.

BUSINESSPer business · holistic instance, all departments, full agentic stack. Default for founders who want one partner across everything.
DEPARTMENTPer department · marketing-only, CS-only, ops-only. Wedge for larger orgs that want to start small.
ROLEPer role · Head of Brand persona, Head of CS persona, Head of Performance persona. Wedge for individual operators inside larger orgs.

Hive Mind hookback.

With customer consent, Atlas instances feed anonymized signal back to Disruptive's Hive Mind. The customer's instance gets smarter as the network grows. The network gets smarter as customers grow. The moat compounds.

POSITIONING
Atlas is the best business partner ever.

Not a tool. Not a copilot. A partner. With memory, reasoning, intuition, opinions, and authorization to act inside guardrails the operator sets.
