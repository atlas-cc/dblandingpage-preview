01 / What it runs

Four layers.
One control center.

Atlas is not a bundle of third-party tools with a logo on top. It is infrastructure we own end-to-end and deploy into your cloud.

01 · AGENTS

Agents that do the work.

Specialized agents for paid media, lifecycle, CX, ops, and research. Each agent is trained on your data, scoped to your tools, and reports into the Control Center.

Built-in: media buyer, lifecycle operator, CX triage, ops bot, research analyst.
02 · MEMORY

Memory that compounds.

Every interaction, campaign, and decision is written to a persistent tenant-isolated store. Atlas retrieves the right context per task. The system gets sharper every week.

Stored per tenant: campaigns, creative, CRM events, ops logs, docs.
03 · BOTS

Bots that talk.

Chat, SMS, and voice surfaces for inbound qualification, support, and internal ops. Deployed on your domain, tuned to your tone, escalated to a human when confidence drops.

Channels: web chat, SMS, WhatsApp, voice, Slack.
04 · CONTROL CENTER

A dashboard you actually read.

Funnels, spend, agent status, anomalies, retrain cadence. One surface. Built for operators who want answers in ten seconds, not a weekly deck.

Built for: founders, ops leads, heads of growth.

02 / Security and compliance

Your data, your cloud.

Atlas is isolated by default. Every tenant gets its own AWS footprint, its own keys, its own memory store. Nothing crosses a tenant boundary.

Infrastructure

Per-tenant AWS.

Dedicated VPC, dedicated databases, dedicated memory. Deployed into your account or ours. You own the keys. Data never leaves the tenant.

Compliance

On the SOC 2 track.

Type I complete. Type II observation in progress. Policies, monitoring, and incident response audited quarterly. Full letter on request under NDA.

Isolation

Dedicated by default.

No shared model inference, no shared vectors, no shared tenancy. Per-tenant encryption at rest and in transit. Model calls scoped to tenant memory only.
