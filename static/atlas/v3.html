§ 03 The Team

A specialist team. On staff from day one.

Five named agents that show up Monday morning. Configured to your business, your tools, your taxonomy, your voice. Not generic copilots. Roles.

Spec. 01
Chief of StaffInbox · calendar · briefs
Owns the inbox and the calendar. Drafts replies in your voice, books meetings, chases the people you forgot to chase. Decides what reaches you and what doesn't.
Sample · 06:14

Replied to fourteen emails, booked Tuesday with the Northwind board, pushed Q3 review to Thursday, flagged one thread that needs your eyes.

Spec. 02
Finance AgentInvoices · AR/AP · runway
Reconciles invoices across the portfolio. Chases late payers in your tone. Watches runway. Tells you the number you need, when you need it, in the format you want it.
Sample · 13:48

Reconciled 142 invoices, zero exceptions, DSO down four days. Three vendors auto-paid under your $5K policy. Two flagged for review. Runway: nineteen months.

Spec. 03
Market AgentPipeline · forecast · accounts
Watches every deal across every brand. Flags risk before it churns, drafts outreach that lands, refreshes the forecast hourly with deal-level reasoning underneath.
Sample · 09:02

Account at $42K MRR hasn't engaged in 11 days. Drafted re-engagement, proposed a 15-minute call Thursday 4pm. Pipeline now 92% to plan with evidence one click below.

Spec. 04
Operations AgentReports · dashboards · anomalies
Builds the report before it's asked for. Watches the metrics that matter across every entity in the portfolio. Pings only when something is genuinely wrong.
Sample · 17:55

EOD report ready across all five brands. Refund rate spiked Tuesday on the new SKU launch. Root cause traced to the size guide. Ticket already filed.

Spec. 05
AssistantResearch · briefs · concierge
Reads what your competitors ship. Watches the channels you don't have time to watch. Delivers a sourced brief Monday morning, in your house format, ready before the call.
Sample · MON 07:00

Three competitors changed pricing this week. One launched a competing line in your category. Fourteen pages, twenty-two sources cited, on your desk before the call.
